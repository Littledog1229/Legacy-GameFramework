When does the focus update?
 . When an element is added or removed from a focused panel
 . When an element's focusability has been changed, and the parent panel is focused
 . When a panel is added or removed from a focused panel
 . When a panel's focusability has been changed, and the parent panel is focused
 . When the mouse moves
 
When will the focus be updated?
 . The focus will be updated at the end of the current frame (is the focus was marked as "dirty")
